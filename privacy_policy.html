How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Core App Functionality

  • Operating your account and authenticating your identity via Google Sign-In
  • Displaying live scores, match results, and football statistics
  • Processing your daily predictions and calculating your points score
  • Running 1v1 and group challenges between users
  • Managing your FC Coins balance — earning, spending, and transaction history
  • Tracking your tier progression from Rookie to Master
  • Maintaining your prediction streak and Clashers Index
  • Displaying your profile, ranking position, and challenge history

Notifications & Communications

  • Sending push notifications for match kickoffs, goal alerts, and results
  • Notifying you when a friend sends or accepts a challenge
  • Alerting you when prediction points are awarded after a match
  • Sending daily mission reminders and prediction reminders (user-configurable)
  • Notifying you of tier upgrades and milestone achievements

Personalisation

  • Prioritising your favourite team fixtures on the Scores screen
  • Filtering notifications by your preferred leagues and teams
  • Showing your position in global, country, and friends leaderboards

App Improvement

  • Analysing aggregate usage patterns to improve features (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Diagnosing crashes and technical issues
  • Understanding which features drive engagement to prioritise development

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where applicable, we process your data under the following legal bases: contract performance (to deliver the app services you requested), legitimate interests (app improvement, security), consent (optional notifications, rewarded ads), and legal obligation (compliance with applicable laws).

Ad Personalisation & Opt-Out

Google AdMob may use your advertising ID and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by visiting your Android device settings:

  • Go to Settings → Google → Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

You can also reset your advertising ID from the same menu. Opting out does not remove ads — it means ads will be less relevant to your interests.

For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, visit: google.com/policies/privacy/partners

FC Coins & In-App Purchases

FC Coins

FC Coins are a virtual in-app currency with no real-world monetary value. They cannot be transferred between users, exchanged for real money, or redeemed outside of Football Clashers. FC Coins are used solely to participate in challenges and purchase cosmetic items within the app.

In-App Purchases

All in-app purchases (coin packages) are processed exclusively by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or process your payment card information. All payment data is handled by Google in accordance with their privacy policy and payment terms. Purchases are final and non-refundable except as required by applicable law or Google Play's refund policy.

Challenge Economy

Our challenge system uses an entry-fee model. Coins spent to enter a challenge are a participation fee paid to the app — not a stake wagered against another user. Winners receive a reward from the app's coin reserve. This model is designed to be compliant with applicable platform policies and is not a gambling mechanism.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. The following measures are in place:

  • Firebase Security Rules — Firestore rules ensure each user can only read and write their own data. No user can access another user's private predictions, coin balance, or challenge details
  • Authentication — All accounts use Google Sign-In with OAuth 2.0. We never store passwords
  • Encrypted transmission — All data between the app and our servers is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Minimal data collection — We collect only what is necessary to operate the features you use
  • Access control — Administrative access to Firestore is restricted to authorised developers only

While we implement industry-standard security practices, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data using commercially reasonable means.
